I'm Pragadeesh Kumar S, a final-year ECE student with a deep focus on Embedded Systems, IoT, and Firmware Development. I thrive at the intersection of hardware and software — where a few lines of C can bring a sensor to life or make a machine decide in milliseconds.

My hands-on experience spans RTOS-based scheduling, real-time biomedical monitoring, wireless communication protocols, and PCB-level hardware design. I've interned at both a PCB manufacturing company and an RF communications firm — grounding my theoretical knowledge in production-grade practice.

I'm driven by one question: how can embedded intelligence solve problems that matter? Whether it's preventing drunk driving, harvesting ambient energy, or detecting fires before they spread — my projects are built with that mission in mind.

Outside of hardware, I'm equally comfortable writing Python pipelines for sensor data, architecting MQTT-based IoT dashboards, or analysing RF signals on a spectrum analyzer. I believe the best embedded engineers speak both languages — silicon and software.
